Faculty and Staff

Stepping on the campus of Nithya Jyothi School, you enter an extraordinary place for children. It is a place where all of our energies are dedicated to helping children become their best selves. We purposefully create an environment to meet their needs from early childhood to emerging adolescence.

Our Team

The principal is the chief administrator of the school, responsible for setting the strategic direction, ensuring academic excellence, managing staff, and overseeing daily operations. They establish policies, represent the school in the community, and work closely with students, parents, and faculty to maintain a positive learning environment.

Ethical Principles for School Staff and Faculty

  • Integrity and Honesty

    Staff and faculty should act with integrity and honesty in all their professional interactions.

  • Respect and Fairness

    They should honor diverse perspectives, provide equal opportunities, and avoid discrimination or favoritism.

  • Confidentiality

    Staff and faculty must respect the confidentiality of student and staff information.

  • Professionalism

    They should model appropriate behavior, demonstrating commitment and dedication to their roles.

  • Responsibility and Accountability

    Faculty and staff should take responsibility for their actions and continually seek to improve their practice.
